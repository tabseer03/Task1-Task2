Quality Assessment Guide

  • MSE (Mean Squared Error): Lower is better. Measures pixel difference between images.
  • PSNR (Peak Signal-to-Noise Ratio): Higher is better. Measured in dB. >40dB is typically excellent.
  • SSIM (Structural Similarity): Ranges 0-1, with 1 being identical. Measures perceived quality.
